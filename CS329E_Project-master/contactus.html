We here at D and J developers have devoted our time and efforts to find a dynamic solution to solve this problem, Dingl. Dingl is an online platform for users worldwide to explore public restrooms within their own proximity. Users, or self-proclaimed Dinglrs, will be able to rate, comment and check in at various restroom locations across the world. Our first phase in implementation is to create a comprehensive overview of the restroom landscape at the University of Texas at Austin, complete with pictures, reviews and ratings. Our goal in mind is to expand coverage first nationally and then globally whilst providing high-quality information in existing locations. Never again will you have to settle for the subpar public restroom experience with Dingl.
